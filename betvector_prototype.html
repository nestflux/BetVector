Welcome to BetVector
Let's configure your system. First, what's your starting bankroll? This is the total amount you're setting aside for betting. BetVector will calculate stakes as a percentage of this amount.
Step 2 of 5
Staking Method
How should BetVector calculate how much to bet on each value bet?
Flat Stakes (Recommended)
Bet 2% of your bankroll on every qualifying bet. Simple, safe, and easy to track.
Percentage of Bankroll
Bet a fixed % of your current bankroll. Adjusts automatically — bets shrink during losing streaks.
Kelly Criterion (Advanced)
Mathematically optimal staking based on edge size. Only recommended after 500+ bets.
Step 3 of 5
Edge Threshold
BetVector only flags a bet when your model's probability exceeds the bookmaker's implied probability by at least this amount. Higher = fewer but stronger picks.
