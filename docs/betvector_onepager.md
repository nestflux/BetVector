# BetVector

**Your personal football betting analyst -- powered by data, not gut feeling.**

---

## The Problem

Most football bettors operate the same way: scroll through the weekend's fixtures, pick a team that "feels right," place a bet, and move on. By Monday, they can't remember if they're up or down for the month. They have no system, no records, and no real edge. Meanwhile, bookmakers employ entire teams of analysts and sophisticated pricing models to make sure the odds are stacked in their favour.

You're essentially bringing a hunch to a data fight.

## What BetVector Does

BetVector is a personal betting intelligence system that watches football data every day and does the hard work for you. It collects match results, team performance stats, and bookmaker odds from public sources across Europe's top leagues. Then it builds its own estimate of what should happen in each upcoming match -- like a weather forecast for football.

When it spots a match where the bookmaker's price doesn't match reality -- say they're offering odds that imply a 55% chance, but the data says it's closer to 70% -- that gap is your advantage. BetVector flags it, tells you exactly how big the edge is, and recommends how much to stake based on your bankroll and risk tolerance.

## How It Works

1. **Collects data daily** -- Match results, team stats, and odds are gathered automatically every morning from trusted public sources.
2. **Analyses every upcoming match** -- The system crunches the numbers for each fixture: recent form, attacking and defensive strength, home advantage, head-to-head history, and more.
3. **Spots mispriced bets** -- It compares its own probability estimates to the bookmaker's odds. When there's a meaningful gap in your favour, it flags a value bet.
4. **Sends you an email** -- Each morning you get a clean summary: which matches have value, how confident the system is, and how much to bet. In the evening, you get your results and running profit.
5. **Learns from its mistakes** -- The system tracks every recommendation, measures its own accuracy over time, and automatically adjusts when it drifts off course.

## What Makes It Different

- **It thinks in probabilities, not predictions.** BetVector doesn't tell you who will win. It tells you where the bookmaker's price is wrong -- and that's a far more profitable question to answer over hundreds of bets.
- **Everything is tracked and transparent.** Every bet the system recommends is logged, win or lose. You can see your profit over time, which leagues perform best, and whether the system is getting sharper or duller -- all on a private dashboard you check from your phone.
- **It protects your bankroll automatically.** Built-in safety rules cap your maximum bet size, pause betting if you hit a losing streak, and alert you before a drawdown gets dangerous. No emotional decisions at 11pm on a Saturday.
- **It runs on autopilot.** Once set up, the entire system runs daily without you touching it. Data collection, analysis, predictions, emails, and result tracking all happen automatically.

## What's Next

BetVector currently covers the Premier League with plans to expand across Europe's top divisions -- including leagues where bookmakers are less accurate and edges are larger. Additional prediction models will be layered in over time, each one sharpening the system's accuracy. The goal is simple: a growing track record over thousands of bets that proves whether the edge is real -- with the data to back it up.
