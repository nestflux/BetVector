The Problem

Why 97% of bettors lose money

The bookmaker's entire business model is designed to ensure you lose. Here's how.

🎰
The House Edge (Overround)
Bookmakers set odds so implied probabilities sum to 105–110%. That extra 5–10% is their guaranteed margin on every market, every match, every day. You're starting behind before you even place a bet.
~5–10%
🧠
Gut Feel vs Data
Most bettors pick based on instinct, bias, or what the TV pundits say. They don't calculate probabilities. They don't compare their assessment to the bookmaker's. They guess — and the house always wins against guesswork.
0 edge
📉
No Performance Tracking
Without tracking your bets systematically — ROI, Brier score, calibration, CLV — you have no idea if your approach works. You can't improve what you can't measure. Most bettors have no idea what their actual win rate is.
blind
The Solution

BetVector: your personal
quant betting system

A complete intelligence system that does the work a sports trading desk does — data collection, statistical modelling, value detection, bankroll management — but built by you, for you.

📡
Automated Data Pipeline
Scrapes results, xG, odds from 3 sources every day. 5 seasons of historical data across multiple leagues. You never manually collect data again.
🧮
Probability Models
Poisson regression generates a scoreline probability matrix for every match — the exact probability of every possible score from 0-0 to 6-6. All markets (1X2, O/U, BTTS) derived from one matrix.
💡
Value Detection
Compares your model's probabilities to every bookmaker's odds. Flags bets only when the edge exceeds your threshold. No edge, no bet — your bankroll thanks you.
🛡️
Bankroll Management
Three staking methods (flat, percentage, Kelly), daily loss limits, drawdown alerts, and a minimum bankroll floor. The system protects you from yourself.
How It Works

From raw data to actionable edge

Every morning at 6 AM, BetVector runs automatically. By 7 AM, you have an email with today's value bets.

📥
Step 1 — 06:00
Scrape & Load
Pull latest results, xG, team stats from Football-Data.co.uk and FBref. Fetch live odds from API-Football. Load everything into the database.
⚙️
Step 2 — 06:02
Compute Features
Calculate rolling form, xG trends, H2H records, rest days, venue stats. 30+ features per team, computed with strict temporal integrity — never leaking future data.
🧠
Step 3 — 06:05
Predict Scorelines
Poisson model generates a 7×7 probability matrix for every upcoming match. All market probabilities — 1X2, Over/Under, BTTS — derived from this single matrix.
💡
Step 4 — 06:06
Find Value Bets
Compare model probabilities to bookmaker odds across every market. Flag bets where edge exceeds your threshold (default: 5%). Calculate optimal stakes.
📧
Step 5 — 07:00
Deliver Picks
Morning email with today's value bets. Open the dashboard for deep dives. Evening email with results and P&L. Weekly summary with calibration and model health.
