Turn football data into mathematical edge
BetVector is a self-built system that scrapes football data, builds probability models, and tells you exactly when the bookmaker's price is wrong — so you only bet when the maths is in your favour. It runs automatically, costs nothing, and gets smarter every week.
The Problem
~7%
House Edge
Bookmakers set odds so implied probabilities sum to 105–110%. You're starting behind before you even bet.
0 edge
Gut Feel Betting
Most bettors pick on instinct, not calculated probability. They never compare their view to the bookmaker's price.
blind
No Tracking
Without tracking ROI, calibration, and CLV systematically, you can't know if your approach works or improve it.
The Solution
📡
Automated Data Pipeline
Scrapes results, xG, and odds from 3 sources daily. 5 seasons of historical data. Runs on GitHub Actions — $0 cost.
🧮
Probability Models
Poisson regression generates a 7×7 scoreline matrix per match. All markets — 1X2, O/U, BTTS — derived from one matrix.
💡
Value Detection
Compares model probs to bookmaker odds across every market. Only flags bets where edge exceeds your threshold.
🛡️
Bankroll Protection
Three staking methods, daily loss limits, drawdown alerts, and a 500-bet paper trading requirement before real money.
