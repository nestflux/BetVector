Executive Summary

PC-24 proved that the same optimization lever can be poison for one league and gold for another. Pinnacle-only filtering improved LaLiga by +21pp and Ligue1 by +22pp, yet destroyed EPL and Championship performance. This plan introduces per-league strategy profiles: each league gets its own optimized combination of edge threshold, sharp filtering, stake weighting, and exposure limits — operating as independent units within one unified system.

Research Foundations

1. Market Efficiency Is Not Uniform

Academic research (University of Reading, 16,000+ English football matches) quantifies the betting volume gap:

LeagueAvg Volume / Match% of EPLEdge Persistence
EPL£23,262100%Hours
Championship£3,04413%1-3 seasons
League One£1,0304.5%Multiple seasons

Lower liquidity = fewer sharp bettors correcting mispricings = larger, more persistent edges. This explains why Championship is profitable.

2. CLV Converges 40× Faster Than ROI

ROI Significance
~2,000 bets
4-5 years at current volume
CLV Significance
~50 bets
2-3 months at current volume

CLV (Closing Line Value) measures whether your bet beat the closing line. Professional operations use CLV as their primary edge metric because it converges 40× faster than ROI.

3. Calibration-Based Triggers, Not ROI-Based

A 2024 paper (Wilkens, Machine Learning with Applications) found that model selection by calibration produced +34.69% ROI, while selection by raw accuracy produced -35.17% ROI. All strategy adjustments must be driven by calibration metrics (Brier score, CLV), never short-term ROI.

4. Daily Exposure Caps Are Non-Negotiable

Current Gap

BetVector has a 5% per-bet cap but no aggregate daily cap. With 10 value bets in one day, 50% of bankroll could be deployed simultaneously. Professional operations cap daily exposure at 15-20%.

League Strategy Profiles in Config

Highest-impact change. LaLiga and Ligue1 get sharp_only: true (Pinnacle comparison only). All others stay false. Modify ValueFinder.find_value_bets() to read per-league strategy config.

PC-25-03
Aggregate Daily Exposure Cap
Add max_daily_exposure: 0.15 (15% of bankroll) and max_league_exposure: 0.08 (8% per league) to safety config. Enforced in BankrollManager.calculate_stake().
PC-25-04
CLV Tracking Infrastructure
Add clv column to value_bets table. Capture closing odds during evening pipeline. Compute CLV = (closing_implied / opening_implied) - 1. Track per-league CLV trend on dashboard.
PC-25-05
Backtest Validation
6-league backtest with LaLiga + Ligue1 sharp_only: true. Compute CI on improvement. Verify no league regresses >5pp. Gate for deploying PC-25-02 live.
PC-25-06
Raise profitable_min_bets to 250
At n=100, there's a 37% chance of showing negative ROI with a true +5% edge. Update settings.yaml market feedback threshold to 250.
PC-25-07
Integration Tests
Test config loading, exposure cap enforcement, CLV computation, strategy profile application. All Phase 1 components covered.

Weight flat stakes by tier: 🟢 1.5×, 🟡 1.0×, 🔴 0.5×, ⚪ 0.5×. Simple multipliers on flat stake — NOT Kelly. Championship at 1.5×, Bundesliga at 0.5×.

New "League Strategy" section on Model Health page: strategy profiles, CLV trends, league × market heatmap, strategy suggestions (display only).
PC-25-11

Extend Sunday pipeline: recompute tiers + CLV, flag tier transitions in weekly email, suggest strategy changes (never auto-apply).

4-week shadow mode before any live strategy change. Track shadow PnL alongside real PnL. Promote only if shadow outperforms by >3pp ROI.

What This Plan Does NOT Do

ExcludedWhy
Separate bankroll pools per leagueToo complex. Stake multipliers achieve 90% of the value.

Automatic bet suppressionMP: "recommend, don't force." Warnings, not exclusion.
ROI-based strategy changesToo noisy at current volumes. Use CLV and Brier instead.
