The Problem

Most football bettors pick teams that "feel right," place a bet, and move on. By Monday, they can't remember if they're up or down for the month. No system. No records. No real edge.

Meanwhile, bookmakers employ entire teams of analysts and sophisticated pricing models to make sure the odds are stacked in their favour.

You're essentially bringing a hunch to a data fight.
What BetVector Does

BetVector is a personal betting intelligence system that watches football data every day and does the hard work for you. It collects match results, team performance stats, and bookmaker odds from public sources across Europe's top leagues.

Then it builds its own estimate of what should happen in each upcoming match — like a weather forecast for football.

How It Works

Collects data daily

Every morning, match results, team stats, and bookmaker odds are gathered automatically from trusted public sources.

Analyses every match

The system crunches the numbers: recent form, attacking and defensive strength, home advantage, head-to-head history, and more.

Spots mispriced bets

It compares its own probability estimates to the bookmaker's odds. When there's a meaningful gap in your favour, it flags a value bet.

Sends you an email

Each morning: which matches have value, how confident the system is, and how much to bet. Each evening: your results and running profit.

Learns from its mistakes

Every recommendation is tracked. The system measures its own accuracy over time and automatically adjusts when it drifts off course.

What Makes It Different

Probabilities, not predictions

It doesn't tell you who will win. It tells you where the bookmaker's price is wrong — a far more profitable question over hundreds of bets.

Fully transparent

Every bet is logged, win or lose. See your profit over time, which leagues work best, and whether the system is getting sharper — on a private dashboard.

Bankroll protection

Built-in safety rules cap bet sizes, pause betting during losing streaks, and alert you before drawdowns get dangerous. No emotional decisions.

Runs on autopilot

Once set up, the entire system runs daily without you touching it. Data, analysis, predictions, emails, and tracking — all automatic.

BetVector currently covers the Premier League with plans to expand across Europe's top divisions — including leagues where bookmakers are less accurate and edges are larger. Additional prediction models will be layered in over time, each one sharpening accuracy. The goal is simple: a growing track record over thousands of bets that proves the edge is real.
